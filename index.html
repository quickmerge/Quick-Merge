Tool Guides

Welcome to the official guides for Quick Merge tools. Here you'll find detailed instructions, benefits, and tips for using each of our powerful, privacy-focused file utilities to streamline your workflow.

Merge PDF Guide

About this Tool

The Merge PDF tool is designed to combine multiple separate PDF files into a single, organized document. This is incredibly useful for professionals, students, and anyone needing to consolidate reports, presentations, research papers, or invoices. By merging files, you simplify sharing, archiving, and printing. The process is handled entirely in your browser, ensuring that your sensitive documents are never uploaded to a server, providing absolute privacy.

How to Use

  1. Click the "Use Tool" button on the Merge PDF card on the homepage to reveal the interface.
  2. Drag and drop your PDF files into the designated upload area, or click the area to open a file browser and select them from your computer. You can select multiple files at once.
  3. The selected files will appear in a list. You can reorder them by clicking and dragging each file to its desired position in the final document.
  4. Once you are satisfied with the order, click the "Merge PDFs" button.
  5. The tool will process the files, and your new, merged PDF will be automatically downloaded to your device, typically named `merged.pdf`.

Split PDF Guide

About this Tool

The Split PDF tool provides the flexibility to extract specific pages from a large PDF document. Whether you need to isolate a single chapter, separate an invoice from a statement, or create smaller, more manageable files, this tool is the perfect solution. All splitting operations are performed locally in your browser, guaranteeing that your document content remains confidential and secure.

How to Use

  1. Click "Use Tool" on the Split PDF card to open the tool.
  2. Upload your PDF file by dragging it into the upload area or by clicking to select it.
  3. In the "Pages to extract" text field, specify the pages you want. You can enter individual pages separated by commas (e.g., `1, 5, 10`) or page ranges using a hyphen (e.g., `3-7`). You can also combine these formats (e.g., `1, 3-5, 9`).
  4. Click the "Split PDF" button to begin the extraction process.
  5. A new PDF containing only your selected pages will be created and downloaded to your device.

Compress PDF Guide

Our Compress PDF tool reduces the file size of your PDF documents without significantly compromising visual quality. This is ideal for emailing large files, uploading to websites with size restrictions, or saving storage space. The tool intelligently optimizes images and removes redundant data, all within the security of your browser.

How to Use

  1. Select the tool and upload your PDF.
  2. Click "Compress PDF". The tool automatically applies a balanced compression level.
  3. Your smaller, optimized PDF will be downloaded instantly.

Unlock PDF Guide

Remove password protection from PDF files that you have the password for but need to remove restrictions for editing or printing. This tool is perfect for when you've received a protected PDF but need to make changes or share it without restrictions.

How to Use

  1. Open the tool and upload your password-protected PDF.
  2. Enter the password for the PDF file.
  3. Click "Unlock PDF" to remove the password protection.
  4. Your unlocked PDF will be downloaded automatically.

Image to PDF Guide

Convert your JPG and PNG images into a universally accessible PDF file. This is perfect for creating portfolios, archiving scanned receipts, or compiling a photo album into a single file for easy sharing. Each image will be placed on its own page in the PDF, maintaining its original quality.

How to Use

  1. Open the tool and upload one or more image files (JPG or PNG).
  2. The tool will list the images you've selected.
  3. Click "Convert to PDF".
  4. A single PDF containing all your images will be created and downloaded.

Text to PDF Guide

Quickly convert any plain text into a clean, professional PDF document. This tool is excellent for saving notes, code snippets, or any text-based content in a more permanent and widely accepted format. You can even use the AI enhancement feature to improve your text's grammar and clarity.

How to Use

  1. Open the tool and paste or type your text into the text area.
  2. For improved text, click "Enhance with AI" (optional).
  3. Click "Convert to PDF" to generate and download your PDF.

PDF to Word Guide

Extract the text content from a PDF file and convert it into an editable Word document (.docx). This is invaluable for repurposing content, editing documents that were originally created as PDFs, or making changes to files where the original source is unavailable.

How to Use

  1. Open the tool and upload your PDF file.
  2. Click "Convert to Word".
  3. A .docx file containing the extracted text will be downloaded.

Sign PDF Guide

Add your digital signature to any PDF document directly in your browser. This eliminates the need to print, sign, and scan documents, saving time and resources. It's perfect for signing contracts, agreements, or forms electronically.

How to Use

  1. Upload the PDF you wish to sign.
  2. Specify the page number where the signature should be placed.
  3. Draw your signature using your mouse or finger on the canvas provided.
  4. Click "Save Signature" to store it for this session.
  5. Click "Sign PDF" to apply the signature and download the signed document.

Compress Images Guide

Reduce the file size of your images (JPG, PNG, WebP) to make them load faster on websites, take up less storage, and be easier to share via email or messaging apps. The tool applies smart compression to minimize quality loss while maximizing size reduction.

How to Use

  1. Open the tool and upload one or more image files.
  2. Click "Compress Images".
  3. A ZIP file containing all your compressed images will be downloaded.

QR Code Generator Guide

Create custom QR codes for URLs, text, contact information, or any other data you want to share quickly. These codes can be scanned by any smartphone camera, making them perfect for marketing materials, business cards, or personal projects.

How to Use

  1. Enter the content (e.g., a website URL) in the text field.
  2. Adjust the size of the QR code if needed.
  3. The QR code will be generated automatically.
  4. Click "Download QR Code" to save it as a PNG image.

About Quick Merge

Our Mission

Quick Merge was founded with a simple but powerful goal: to provide fast, reliable, and completely private file processing tools for everyone. In an era of increasing digital surveillance and data breaches, we believe that your files should remain yours alone. That's why all our tools run directly in your browser—your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Why Choose Us?

  • Privacy-First: Your files are processed locally in your browser. We never upload, store, or access your data.
  • Free & Accessible: All our tools are completely free to use, with no hidden fees, subscriptions, or usage limits.
  • No Registration Required: Use our tools instantly without creating an account or providing any personal information.
  • Secure by Design: Since processing happens locally, there's no risk of server-side data breaches or unauthorized access.
  • Cross-Platform: Our tools work on any device with a modern web browser—Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, or Android.

Our Technology

Quick Merge leverages cutting-edge web technologies like JavaScript libraries (pdf-lib, jsPDF, qrcode.js) to perform complex file operations directly in your browser. This client-side processing approach not only guarantees your privacy but also delivers incredibly fast performance since there's no need to upload files to a server and wait for processing.
