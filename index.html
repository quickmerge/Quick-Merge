About Quick Merge

Our Mission

Quick Merge was created with a simple goal: to provide powerful, privacy-focused file processing tools that work directly in your browser. We believe that you shouldn't have to compromise your privacy or security to get the tools you need.

Unlike many online services, we never upload your files to our servers. All processing happens locally on your device, ensuring complete privacy and security. Our mission is to democratize access to powerful file processing tools while maintaining the highest standards of user privacy.

Our Technology

We leverage modern web technologies like WebAssembly, JavaScript libraries, and browser APIs to bring you desktop-level functionality without requiring any downloads or installations. Our platform is built on cutting-edge web standards that ensure compatibility across all modern browsers.

Our tools are built with performance in mind, ensuring that even complex operations like PDF manipulation and image processing happen quickly and efficiently. We continuously update our technology stack to incorporate the latest advancements in browser capabilities.

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Privacy: Your files never leave your device. We believe privacy is a fundamental right, not a premium feature.
  • Security: No data collection or tracking. We don't store your files or personal information.
  • Accessibility: Free tools available to everyone, regardless of technical expertise or financial means.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving and adding new features based on user feedback.
  • User Experience: Intuitive interfaces that anyone can use without a learning curve.

Our Story

Quick Merge was founded in July 2024 by a team of developers who were frustrated with the limitations of existing online file tools. We noticed that most services required uploading sensitive files to remote servers, creating privacy concerns and potential security risks.

We set out to create a better solution - a suite of tools that would process files entirely in the browser, eliminating the need for file uploads while maintaining full functionality. Today, Quick Merge serves thousands of users worldwide who trust us with their file processing needs.

Our journey began with simple PDF merging capabilities and has expanded to include over 15 different tools covering PDF manipulation, image processing, text conversion, and utility functions. We're proud to have created a platform that puts user privacy first without compromising on functionality.

Our Values

At Quick Merge, we operate based on a few core principles:

  • Privacy by Design: We build our tools with privacy as a fundamental requirement, not an afterthought.
  • Transparency: We're open about how our tools work and what data we collect (which is minimal).
  • Accessibility: We believe powerful tools should be available to everyone, regardless of technical expertise.
  • Continuous Improvement: We regularly update our tools based on user feedback and technological advancements.
  • Community Focus: We listen to our users and prioritize features that provide the most value to our community.

Our Team

Quick Merge is developed by a dedicated team of software engineers, UX designers, and product managers who are passionate about creating tools that make people's lives easier. Our team has extensive experience in web development, security, and user experience design.

We're a distributed team working from different parts of the world, united by our shared vision of creating privacy-focused, accessible tools for everyone. Our diverse backgrounds and perspectives help us create products that work for users across different regions and use cases.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit certain websites. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Cookies set by the website owner are called "first party cookies". Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called "third party cookies". Third party cookies enable third party features or functionality to be provided on or through the website.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.
  • Preference Cookies: These enable the website to remember information that changes the way the site behaves or looks, like your preferred language or region.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

We use both session cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your device for a set period of time or until you delete them).

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that place cookies on your device. These include:

  • Google Analytics: To understand how users interact with our website
  • Google AdSense: To display relevant advertisements

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and cookie policies. We encourage you to review these policies to understand how these companies use your information.

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.

Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.aboutcookies.org or www.allaboutcookies.org.
