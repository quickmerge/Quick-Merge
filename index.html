Tool Guides

Welcome to the official guides for Quick Merge tools. Here you'll find detailed instructions, benefits, and tips for using each of our powerful, privacy-focused file utilities to streamline your workflow.

Merge PDF Guide

About this Tool

The Merge PDF tool is designed to combine multiple separate PDF files into a single, organized document. This is incredibly useful for professionals, students, and anyone needing to consolidate reports, presentations, research papers, or invoices. By merging files, you simplify sharing, archiving, and printing. The process is handled entirely in your browser, ensuring that your sensitive documents are never uploaded to a server, providing absolute privacy.

How to Use

  1. Click the "Use Tool" button on the Merge PDF card on the homepage to reveal the interface.
  2. Drag and drop your PDF files into the designated upload area, or click the area to open a file browser and select them from your computer. You can select multiple files at once.
  3. The selected files will appear in a list. You can reorder them by clicking and dragging each file to its desired position in the final document.
  4. Once you are satisfied with the order, click the "Merge PDFs" button.
  5. The tool will process the files, and your new, merged PDF will be automatically downloaded to your device, typically named `merged.pdf`.

Split PDF Guide

About this Tool

The Split PDF tool provides the flexibility to extract specific pages from a large PDF document. Whether you need to isolate a single chapter, separate an invoice from a statement, or create smaller, more manageable files, this tool is the perfect solution. All splitting operations are performed locally in your browser, guaranteeing that your document content remains confidential and secure.

How to Use

  1. Click "Use Tool" on the Split PDF card to open the tool.
  2. Upload your PDF file by dragging it into the upload area or by clicking to select it.
  3. In the "Pages to extract" text field, specify the pages you want. You can enter individual pages separated by commas (e.g., `1, 5, 10`) or page ranges using a hyphen (e.g., `3-7`). You can also combine these formats (e.g., `1, 3-5, 9`).
  4. Click the "Split PDF" button to begin the extraction process.
  5. A new PDF containing only your selected pages will be created and downloaded to your device.

Compress PDF Guide

Our Compress PDF tool reduces the file size of your PDF documents without significantly compromising visual quality. This is ideal for emailing large files, uploading to websites with size restrictions, or saving storage space. The tool intelligently optimizes images and removes redundant data, all within the security of your browser.

How to Use

  1. Select the tool and upload your PDF.
  2. Click "Compress PDF". The tool automatically applies a balanced compression level.
  3. Your smaller, optimized PDF will be downloaded instantly.

Unlock PDF Guide

Remove password protection from PDF files that you have the password for but need to remove restrictions for editing or printing. This tool is perfect for when you've received a protected PDF but need to make changes or share it without restrictions.

How to Use

  1. Open the tool and upload your password-protected PDF.
  2. Enter the password for the PDF file.
  3. Click "Unlock PDF" to remove the password protection.
  4. Your unlocked PDF will be downloaded automatically.

Image to PDF Guide

Convert your JPG and PNG images into a universally accessible PDF file. This is perfect for creating portfolios, archiving scanned receipts, or compiling a photo album into a single file for easy sharing. Each image will be placed on its own page in the PDF, maintaining its original quality.

How to Use

  1. Open the tool and upload one or more image files (JPG or PNG).
  2. The tool will list the images you've selected.
  3. Click "Convert to PDF".
  4. A single PDF containing all your images will be created and downloaded.

Text to PDF Guide

Quickly convert any plain text into a clean, professional PDF document. This tool is excellent for turning notes, articles, or any text content into a more formal, shareable format. An AI enhancement feature is also available to help improve the structure and grammar of your text.

How to Use

  1. Open the tool and paste or type your text into the text area.
  2. Click "Convert to PDF" to create a PDF from your text.
  3. Optionally, use the "Enhance with AI" button to improve your text before conversion.

PDF to Word Guide

Convert your PDF documents into editable Word files. This tool extracts the text content from your PDF and creates a .docx file that you can open and edit in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or other word processors.

How to Use

  1. Open the tool and upload your PDF file.
  2. Click "Convert to Word".
  3. The tool will extract the text and create a downloadable .docx file.

Sign PDF Guide

Add your digital signature to PDF documents quickly and securely. This tool allows you to draw your signature directly in the browser and place it on any page of your PDF, perfect for signing contracts, forms, or agreements without needing to print and scan.

How to Use

  1. Upload the PDF you need to sign.
  2. Specify the page number where the signature should appear.
  3. Draw your signature in the canvas area and save it.
  4. Click "Sign PDF" to apply the signature and download the signed document.

Compress Images Guide

Reduce the file size of your images (JPEG, PNG, WebP) while maintaining good visual quality. This tool is perfect for optimizing images for websites, social media, or email attachments, helping pages load faster and saving bandwidth.

How to Use

  1. Upload one or more image files.
  2. Click "Compress Images".
  3. The tool will process each image and provide a download link for the compressed version.

QR Code Generator Guide

Create custom QR codes for URLs, text, contact information, or any other data. Our generator allows you to customize the size and colors of your QR code, making it perfect for marketing materials, presentations, or personal use.

How to Use

  1. Enter the content (URL, text, etc.) for your QR code.
  2. Adjust the size if needed.
  3. The QR code will be generated automatically.
  4. Click "Download QR Code" to save it as a PNG image.

About Quick Merge

Quick Merge was born from a simple idea: powerful file processing tools should be fast, free, and respect your privacy. In a digital world where sensitive documents are often uploaded to unknown servers, we offer a refreshing alternative.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and professionals with a comprehensive suite of file processing tools that are not only efficient and easy to use but also guarantee complete privacy by running entirely within the user's browser. We believe you shouldn't have to sacrifice security for convenience.

How It Works: Privacy by Design

Unlike many online file converters and processors, Quick Merge tools run 100% locally in your browser. When you use our tools:

  • Your Files Never Leave Your Computer: All processing happens on your device. We don't have servers to upload your files to, which means there is zero risk of your sensitive documents being intercepted, stored, or misused by us or any third party.
  • No Account Required: Use our tools instantly without signing up for an account. We don't track your usage or store any personal data.
  • Fast and Efficient: By eliminating the upload and download time to a remote server, our tools are often faster than cloud-based alternatives, especially for smaller files.

The Technology Behind the Tools

Quick Merge is built using modern web technologies, primarily JavaScript, and leverages powerful, open-source libraries like PDF-LIB.js for PDF manipulation. This allows us to provide robust functionality directly in the browser without compromising on performance or security.

Our Commitment

We are committed to keeping Quick Merge tools free and accessible. We may display non-intrusive advertisements to help cover development and maintenance costs, but we will never sell user data or compromise on our core principle of privacy-first design.
